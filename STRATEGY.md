# AHK Design Academy - Website Strategy & Structure

This document outlines the complete production-ready plan for the AHK Design Academy website.

---

## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
- **Brand Name:** AHK Design Academy
- **Industry:** Online Design Education (Architecture, Interior Design, Visualization)
- **Primary Offering:** Training in SketchUp, Enscape, V-Ray, D5 Render, Twinmotion
- **Target Audience:** University students, beginners, freelancers, young professionals in Kenya.
- **Tone:** Modern, Premium, Clean, Professional, Design-focused.

## 4. COURSE STRUCTURE

### 1. Full Program (KSh 30,000)
- **Description:** The ultimate workflow from 2D/3D modeling to high-end visualization.
- **Modules:**
  - Week 1-2: Advanced SketchUp Modeling (Architecture & Interiors)
  - Week 3: Interior Design Detailing & Furniture Modeling
  - Week 4: Lighting Principles & Material Application
  - Week 5-6: Rendering with Enscape & V-Ray
  - Week 7: Post-production in Photoshop & D5 Render
  - Week 8: Final Project & Portfolio Review
- **Duration:** 8 Weeks
- **Tools:** SketchUp, Enscape, V-Ray, D5, Photoshop.

### 2. Modeling Only (KSh 20,000)
- **Description:** Focus on precision modeling for architecture and interior design.
- **Modules:**
  - Week 1: SketchUp Interface & Basic Tools
  - Week 2: Architectural Modeling (Walls, Openings, Roofs)
  - Week 3: Interior Modeling & Component Management
  - Week 4: Basic Steel Structures & Site Modeling
  - Week 5: Organizing for Rendering (Layers/Tags)
  - Week 6: Exporting & Documentation
- **Duration:** 6 Weeks
- **Tools:** SketchUp.

### 3. Rendering Only (KSh 10,000)
- **Description:** Transform your models into photorealistic masterpieces.
- **Modules:**
  - Week 1: Introduction to Real-time Rendering (Enscape)
  - Week 2: V-Ray Fundamentals (Lighting & Global Illumination)
  - Week 3: Advanced Materials & Texturing
  - Week 4: D5 Render & Twinmotion for Animation
  - Week 5: Exterior vs. Interior Lighting Setups
  - Week 6: Post-processing & Visual Storytelling
- **Duration:** 6 Weeks
- **Tools:** Enscape, V-Ray, D5, Twinmotion.

## 5. UI/UX DESIGN SYSTEM

### Colors
- **Primary:** #1A1A1A (Deep Charcoal - Professional)
- **Secondary:** #F5F5F5 (Light Gray - Clean)
- **Accent:** #D4AF37 (Gold - Premium/Elite) or #FF6B00 (Vibrant Orange - Energy)
- **Background:** #FFFFFF (White)
- **Text:** #333333 (Dark Gray)

### Typography
- **Headings:** 'Inter' or 'Space Grotesk' (Bold, 700)
- **Body:** 'Inter' (Regular, 400)
- **Scale:** H1 (48px), H2 (36px), H3 (24px), Body (16px), Small (14px)

### Components
- **Buttons:** Rounded (8px), Solid Primary, Ghost Secondary.
- **Cards:** Subtle shadow (0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)), 12px border radius.
- **Forms:** Minimalist, floating labels.

## 6. CONVERSION SYSTEM
- **CTA Placements:** Sticky "Enroll Now" in Nav, Hero, After every 2 sections, Footer.
- **WhatsApp:** Floating button on bottom right with pre-filled: "Hi AHK, I'm interested in the [Course Name]..."
- **Urgency:** "Next Intake: April 5th - Only 4 Slots Left!"
- **Lead Magnet:** "Free 3D Modeling Checklist" PDF in exchange for email.
